## Overview

N3L is an innovative financial management application that combines the power of voice interaction through the Omi device with advanced AI to provide personalized financial advice and spending tracking. By simply speaking your purchases and expenses into the Omi device, N3L builds a comprehensive understanding of your financial habits and provides actionable insights to help you achieve your financial goals.

## How It Works

1. **Voice Input**: Users speak their transactions into the Omi device
   - "I just spent $45 on groceries at Walmart"
   - "Paid $60 for gas today"

2. **AI Processing**: The system processes and categorizes transactions
   - Automatic categorization of expenses
   - Pattern recognition in spending habits
   - Context building for personalized advice

3. **Smart Analysis**: AI analyzes spending patterns and provides insights
   - Identifies areas of overspending
   - Suggests potential savings opportunities
   - Tracks progress towards financial goals

4. **Interactive Advice**: Users can ask specific questions
   - "How can I save $200 this month?"
   - "Where am I overspending?"
   - "What's my biggest expense category?"
